AI Contract Review Automation

Upload a contract → get a full risk & clause report in minutes

What it does

Accepts uploaded contracts (PDF or Doc), automatically reviews them using GPT-4, identifies risky clauses and missing provisions, and generates a clear, structured report with specific recommendations for each issue found.

Problem solved

Lawyers and businesses spend hours on first-pass contract reviews. This automation delivers that analysis in minutes, surfacing risks, gaps, and action points — freeing legal professionals for higher-value work.

Tools: OpenAI GPT-4 Make Zapier n8n Google Docs Google Drive PDF Processing
03

Daily Legal Judgment Digest

Fresh case law summaries delivered to your inbox every morning at 7 AM

What it does

Each morning at 7 AM, the workflow searches legal databases for the latest judgments in a chosen practice area, uses AI to summarise the top 3–5 decisions, highlights key developments, and delivers the digest via email, WhatsApp, or Slack.

Problem solved

Staying current with case law means checking multiple databases manually — a significant daily time cost. This automation delivers curated, AI-summarised updates automatically every morning, keeping lawyers informed effortlessly.

Tools: OpenAI GPT-4 Make Zapier n8n Legal APIs Gmail Google Sheets WhatsApp Slack
